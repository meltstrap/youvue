Getting Started

Create a YouVue Account.

Watch videos

  • Search for any video or topic.
  • Click "Browse" to explore YouVue.
  • Choose to view results under Videos, Music, Shows, Movies, or Trailers, and filter by your category of interest.

Tip: Want other browsing options?

  • Once you're on a category page, filter the results further by popularity and date range.
  • Visit YouVue Charts to see a ranked list of popular videos.

Tip: Found a video you like?

  • Click Like and add it to your Favorites so you can easily find it in your account.
  • Share it with your friends on Twitter, Facebook, or other social networks.
  • Subscribe to receive automatic updates from the channel.

Upload a video

  • Upload a file from your computer or record a video using your phone or webcam.
  • Customize your channel's appearance.

Tip: For best results, complete the Settings and Info sections for your video.

  • Share your video publicly or use private sharing to control who can watch it.
  • Include relevant keywords in the Tags section and select the appropriate category.
  • Give the video an accurate title and description to help people discover it.
